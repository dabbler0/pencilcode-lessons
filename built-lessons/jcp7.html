Tick and If

Try this:

tick 60, ->
  fd 5
  rt 2

tick 60 makes the turtle do something 60 times a second, forever. Like with buttons, make sure everything is indented properly.

Tasks

  1. Can you figure out how to make the turtle move faster or slower? Hint: when a tick is running, speed doesn't do anything.
  2. Can you figure out how to make the circle that the turtle goes in bigger or smaller?

Now try this:

tick 60, ->
  if pressed 'up'
    fd 5
  rt 2

Like with for loops, make sure everything is indented properly: notice how fd 10 only happens when the key is pressed, but rt 1 happens all the time.

Tasks

  1. Make a turtle that is controlled by the arrow keys -- move forward, back, and turn left and right.
  2. Be creative with that turtle! Ideas: make the space key give a speed boost
